Here we report a longitudinal study of gene-corrected progenitor cell populations from 8 patients using 454 pyrosequencing to map vector integration sites, and extensive resampling to allow quantification of clonal abundance. The longitudinal analysis emphasizes that key features of transduced cell populations\u2014including diversity, integration site clustering, and expansion of some clones\u2014were established early after transplantation.
