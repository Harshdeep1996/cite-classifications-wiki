The ability of different nodes to predictively discriminate species and the variability of landmark and EFD traits at each node is then analyzed. The first leaves in the series are not only more like each other, but are also less variable across species. From this similar, shared leaf shape, subsequent leaves in the heteroblastic series follow divergent morphological trajectories.
